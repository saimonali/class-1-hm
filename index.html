Rifat murder case: Death-row convict Minni seeks bail from HC

Death-row convict Ayesha Siddika Minni today (October 16, 2022) filed a petition with the High Court seeking bail in the murder case of her husband Rifath Sharif. Minni, now in a Kashimpur jail, submitted the petition through her lawyers ZI Khan Panna and AM Jamiul Hoque Faisal, saying that she was not involved in the murder of Rifat Sharif and that she was trying to save him.
